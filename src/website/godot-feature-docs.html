Quick Start Guide

Install the Godot Addon to get started:

  1. Once in your project, click on AssetLib at the top of the Godot editor.
  2. Click the Import... button.
  3. Select the Folded Paper Engine Godot addon zip file.
  4. Click Open.
  5. Click on the Plugins... button.
  6. Check the box next to Folded Paper Engine to enable it.
  7. Click Close.
  8. Restart Godot.
