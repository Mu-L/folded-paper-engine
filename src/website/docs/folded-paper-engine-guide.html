What each page covers

Gameplay and interactions

  • Players and Characters: see Objects and Characters for tagging players/NPCs, physics bodies, and cameras.
  • Holdables and Inventory: Inventory and Holdables walks through item kinds, slots, pickup/drop, and UI hooks.
  • Triggers and Commands: Triggers and Commands includes step-by-step trigger setup, command chaining, and puzzle patterns.
  • Dialogues: Conversations covers speaker setup, dialogue data, and reply selection.
  • UI: UI and Cursors explains UI options, cursors, and keeping UI elements visible with your camera.

Timing, audio, and scenes

  • Frame Events: Animation and Frame Events shows how to mark frames in Blender and hook them to sounds, doors, or cameras.
  • Audio and Music: Audio explains Speakers, background music, distance falloff, looping, and troubleshooting.
  • Level transitions and streaming: Sub Scenes and Level Loading covers doorways, hubs, and pausing parents while sub-scenes are active.
  • Environment metadata: Scene Settings documents sky color, gravity overrides, background music defaults, and FX toggles.
  • Pipeline and fixes: Pipeline and Runtime plus Troubleshooting explain the Blender ↔ Godot loop and quick checks for stale imports.
