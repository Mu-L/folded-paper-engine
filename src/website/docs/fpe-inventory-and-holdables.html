Inventory and Holdables
Item kinds, slots, hold zones, and deposits

Inventory model

  • Inventory types: player and character inventories use registered size definitions to cap slot counts.
  • Item kinds are registered by ID so triggers and hold zones can spawn the correct InventoryItemSlot when transferring quantities.
  • Player inventory persistence can be toggled; by default it is cleared when the engine unloads unless KeepPlayerInventory is set.

Holdable items

  • Mark an object as Holdable in Blender to allow pickup. Holdables ride physics and use a force-based hold zone rather than teleporting.
  • Player configs that enable CanHoldItems spawn a HoldZone in front of the camera. Adjust distance, size, and optional zone scene for UI or VFX.
  • Hold zones dampen motion, apply sway, and drop items if they stray beyond the maximum range or hit the floor.

Deposits and pickups

  • Triggers can carry inventory slots. When a player enters, the slot auto-adds into the player inventory; overflow remains on the trigger.
  • Hold zones listen for nearby TrackingArea3D instances so you can drop or withdraw items when standing over a deposit volume.
